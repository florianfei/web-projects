LongZhu IM

The mystery of how arguably the strongest roster on paper failed to deliver after two splits of competition in LCK.

"This roster lacks any sort of Synergy" - CaoJungle 2016

On Jan 4th, 2016, Incredible Miracle renamed themselves to Longzhu, who had already been a sponsor of the team. It was announced later in the month that the team would participate with a 10-man roster with big names such as Flame, Expession, CoCo, and Cpt Jack. To see the full history of the team, check the Incredible Miracle page. The current starting roster consists of

  • Expession
  • Chaser
  • Coco
  • Fury
  • Pure

although many members are frequently subbed out for the likes of

  • Flame
  • Crash
  • Frozen
  • Emperor
